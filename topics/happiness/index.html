Topic 5: Happiness

From Epicureans to the present, some philosophers have thought that the meaning of life might lie in seeking happiness. We consider a classic expression of this view in anglophone philosophy, and then some criticisms of it.

Reading

  1. John Stuart Mill, from Utilitarianism.
  2. Robert Nozick

See also

  • David Konstan, “Epicurus” in the SEP
  • Catherine Wilson, “How to be an Epicurean” in Aeon magazine, 2019
