Topic 1: Philosophy

One of the most prominent living philosophers, Martha Nussbaum, was recently asked “How would you define the role of a philosopher in today’s world?” She answered:

I wouldn’t. There are so many good ways to engage with the world, and each philosopher should figure it out for herself. I don’t like telling others what to do.

Still, philosophy has a history. And its history involves people doing specific things and calling them “philosophy,” and not calling other things “philosophy.”) So, it has some boundaries, even if they’re shifting and disputed. We’ll look at some ideas about what philosophy is.
