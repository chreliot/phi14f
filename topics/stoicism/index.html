Topic 4: Stoicism

The next few topics are specific, positive accounts of the meaning of life. The first one is the ancient — and currently popular — view called Stoicism. Coming to understand what is really good, the Stoics argued, and how the nature of goodness and happiness are rooted in nature, could lead us toward perfect lives.
