Test 2 Questions

The questions for the second test will be posted here at the exam time (10:30, Tuesday, December 2). It will be designed to take ou not more than 90 minutes. It will be due, via upload, by 5 PM, on Saturday, December 5.

The test will consist of written questions. You should study by reviewing your notes, and perhaps what you underlined or annotated in the texts. You will be able to use all your notes. However, you shouldn’t plan to rely on external sources — that is, other than the texts assigned for the course and your notes — as you will be required to acknowledge them, and using but not acknowledging them would mean a 0 grade for the test. So, plan to focus on using your notes.
